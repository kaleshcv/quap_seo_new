What services do QUAP offer?
We deliver certified OEM-used auto parts, all over the US. We, at QUAP, ensure that we test each part before dispatch. These used auto parts are sourced from salvage & junkyards, all over the US. Contact us on our toll-free number, 1-833-583-2590, for any assistance or expert opinion.
Which places do you service?
QUAP delivers all over the US. We provide the same services for people living in remote as well as urban places. Our wide network of auto salvage yards brings you a range of parts like never before.
Do you promote any specific junkyards or dealers?
No. QUAP firmly believes in delivering only the best quality parts to your doorstep. Thus, we source the used auto parts for the junkyard or salvage yard that has the best part.
What types of used auto parts do you sell?
We sell a long list of used auto parts online including, radiator, transmission, wheels, turbos, etc. We can even find you used parts like lithium-ion batteries and carbon-fiber parts. Request and you will have it.
How long will you take to find the part?
On average, our team of experts will take 4-7 days to find the part. If we can find the exact used auto part in a salvage yard near you, it will take less time. It does not include the shipping time.
What is your warranty policy?
We have a warranty policy of 90 days. You can return or exchange the product within 90 days after the delivery. In the case of an exchange of a used auto part, there might be a price difference.
Do you sell new auto parts?
Sorry, we don’t. Used auto parts are both affordable and environmentally friendly. QUAP provides a genuine alternative to people who cannot either afford or find the exact auto part. Reusing and recycling will reduce wastage to a great extent.
Do you provide used auto parts for trucks?
Yes, of course. At QUAP, we provide used auto parts for cars, trucks, SUVs, CUVs, sedans, and pick-up trucks.
